Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
+
We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy:
  • Time frame: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags
  • Process: Initiate return through your account or contact support
  • Refund: Full refund to original payment method within 5-7 business days
Exceptions: Sale items (50%+ off) are final sale. Custom/personalized items cannot be returned. 💖
How do I start a return or exchange?
+
Starting a return is super simple:
  • Step 1: Log into your DevDoll account
  • Step 2: Find your order and click "Return Items"
  • Step 3: Select items and reason for return
  • Step 4: Print the prepaid return label
  • Step 5: Pack items and drop off at any post office
No account? Email us at returns@devdoll.com with your order number! 📧

Product Care

How should I care for my DevDoll items?
+
Keep your kawaii items looking perfect with these care tips:
  • Washing: Machine wash cold with like colors
  • Drying: Tumble dry low or hang dry to prevent shrinking
  • Ironing: Iron inside out on low heat to protect prints
  • Storage: Fold neatly or hang to prevent wrinkles
Special tip: Turn graphic tees inside out before washing to keep designs vibrant! ✨
What materials do you use?
+
We use only the softest, highest-quality materials:
  • Cotton Blend: 95% premium cotton, 5% elastane for comfort
  • Eco-friendly: Sustainable and ethically sourced materials
  • Hypoallergenic: Safe for sensitive skin
  • Pre-shrunk: Maintains size and shape after washing
All materials are tested for safety and comfort! 🌿💖
